The Los Angeles Skyline

Back

You will make the Los Angeles Skyline. Click on the squares of 1 and 2 and click on the canvas to add the sky and ground. Next click 3 and start making your city. Then use 2 again to cover up the bottom area of black. Then use 4 for some grass, 5 for some clouds and 6 for the Sun (or Moon)!

1 = Sky

2 = Ground

3 = Buildings

4 = Grass

5 = Clouds

6 = Sun/Moon
